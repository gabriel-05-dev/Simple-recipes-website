Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter & Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning
  • Bread Crumbs

Preparation

The instructions below are designed to show you how to prepare your mac and cheese

  1. Boil the Macaroni, drain and transfer to a baking dish
  2. Make the cheese sauce, and pour the sauce over the noodles
  3. Make the topping and spread it over the mac and cbeese
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden
