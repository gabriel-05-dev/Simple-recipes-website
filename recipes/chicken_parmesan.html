Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast Halves
  • Salt and Black pepper
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Basil
  • Tomato sauce

Preparation

The instructions below are designed to show you how to prepare your chicken parmesan

  1. Preheat the oven, to 230 Degrees(C)
  2. Place the chicken breat, seasoning it with pepper and salt in the baking tray
  3. Beat some eggs in a shallow bowl and coat the chicken breast in flour
  4. Mix crumbs and cheese in a seperate bowl and set aside
  5. Dip the coated chicken in the egg mix, then the bread crumb mixture and cook them in the oven.
  6. After cooking, drouse the chicken breat in parmesan and plate the food
  7. Enjoy!
