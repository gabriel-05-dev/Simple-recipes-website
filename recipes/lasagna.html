Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices, Cheeses
  • Lasagna sheets and an Egg

Preparation

The instructions below are designed to show you how to prepare your lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Bake until golden brown on top
  7. Let the lasagna reset before serving!
